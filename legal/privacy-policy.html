Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 5, 2026

Welcome to Play Lume. Your privacy is critical to our mission. This policy explains how we handle data to provide the ultimate digital board game experience.

1. Data We Collect

Play Lume is designed for family fun and minimizes data collection. We collect the following:

  • Game State Data: Room codes, player nicknames, and in-game inputs (like alibis in "Guess The Liar" or votes in "Most Likely To").
  • Device Information: Basic device type to ensure our Neural UI renders correctly.

2. How We Use Your Data

Data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Real-time Syncing: To ensure all players in a room see the same game state instantly.
  • Gameplay Logic: To determine winners, liars, and statistics within a game session.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase to power our real-time database and hosting. Your data is processed by Firebase according to their security standards. We do not sell your data to third-party advertisers.

4. Data Retention

Game session data is temporary. Once a room is dissolved or the session expires, the temporary game state is cleared from our active memory.

5. Your Rights

Since we do not maintain permanent personal accounts, your data is tied to your current game session. You can leave a game room at any time to stop data transmission.
